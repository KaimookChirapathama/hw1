Welcome to Boonlorm Farm!

Organic dates farm and local produces market in Chiang Mai Thailand

Open everyday from 8 am to 6 pm

Come and be part of the nature at Boonlorm Farm. We are an organic date palm farm located right in the mountain valley of Chiang Mai. Get away from the city hectic and take a sip of orange coffee at Aunya Cafe at the farm. And don't forget to go say hi to our 2 pet cows (Ms. Silver and Mr. Gold).
